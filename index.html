I am a Postdoctoral Researcher in Cognitive Neuroscience at Sapienza University of Rome. My research focuses on memory, misinformation, and the effects of concussions in combat sports.

Research

  • Memory, Misinformation, and Concussions
    Abstract

    My work investigates how hyperrealistic AI-generated faces (deepfakes) influence visual perception, trust, and memory encoding. Through behavioral experiments and recognition paradigms, I explore how authenticity beliefs and affective cues shape cognitive responses.

  • Misinformation Susceptibility and Decision-Making
    Abstract

    This line of research examines how emotional and cognitive factors influence susceptibility to misinformation. Using network psychometrics and path models, I study predictors of belief formation and correction across different contexts such as health and climate communication.
